release300.txt
==============

Release notes for TC4 standard libraries, version 3.00:
 cADC
 cButton
 cLCD
 cmndproc
 mcEEPROM
 PWM16
 thermocouple


Libraries have been updated for compatibility with Arduino 1.0.  They
will also continue to work under previous Arduino releases (0022 has been
tested).

cmdnproc library
----------------
Updated to allow either LF or CR-LF to terminate serial commands.
